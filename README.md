# 🚀 APICA – AI Powered Intelligent Cross-Sell Agent

> Smarter Banking. Personalized by AI.

APICA is an AI-driven intelligent cross-sell recommendation system designed to help banks deliver personalized, timely, and compliant financial product recommendations to customers.

Built for modern digital banking, APICA transforms customer transaction data, life-event signals, and risk profiles into explainable product recommendations — increasing engagement, improving conversion rates, and enhancing customer experience.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Banks today offer multiple financial products (credit cards, loans, insurance, investments), but most customers receive:

- ❌ Generic offers  
- ❌ Poorly timed communication  
- ❌ Irrelevant product suggestions  
- ❌ Low engagement and conversion  

This results in revenue loss and weak customer experience.

APICA solves this using AI-powered personalization with Responsible AI principles.
